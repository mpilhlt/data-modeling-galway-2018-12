Akoma Ntoso

Design Principles

  • separate authoritative and other text
  • track document's lifecycle
  • semantically anonymous
  • agnostic wrt tools / infrastructure

Ontologies

Requirements

learned from CIDOC CRM, ALLOT, UNDO, LKIF-Core, Prov-O, ...

  • Actions & Processes & Agents & Roles ... & temporal indexes
  • Keep track of assertions' provenances
  • Use instances of general classes (try to stay in the ABox)
  • Defeasible logic

Conclusion

Lessons learned

(for legal history)

  • TEI is fine after all
  • In xml, keep analysis in one place
  • There, refer to standard/online taxonomies/thesauri
  • Top-level concepts:
    Documents, persons, organizations, roles, concepts, terms, events
  • ... in xml as well as in ontologies
  • Modeling behavior is still not practical!
